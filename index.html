Quick Help

  • Left click to use current tool.
  • Use Build to place towers and auto-connect walls.
  • Right click or middle click and drag to pan.
  • Shift/Ctrl/Cmd + click to multi-select in Select / Move.
  • Hold Ctrl to temporarily disable object snapping.
  • Ctrl+C copies selection; left click places the ghost copy.
  • Alt + drag rotates a selected group; it snaps near 90 degree angles.
  • Rotating selected walls can snap to aligned external walls.
  • Mouse wheel to zoom at cursor.
